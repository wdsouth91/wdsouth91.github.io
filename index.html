Or Whatever This Is

Good morning, afternoon, or whatever time it might be when you read this. I hope you're doing well. I have been on a strange and long, winding journey these past few weeks as I navigate changing my day job positions around and accepting new challenges.

This has of course, gotten in the way of my keeping up with the NaNoWriMo writing, but the project I started wasn't one that I was truly obsessed with. In rereading some of my older notes to find inspiration I rediscovered my love of prose-poetry.

Prose poetry1 as defined by the poetry foundation is a prose composition that while not broken up into verses like traditional poetry still borrows heavily from the form. Using rhythm or heavy symbology. This reminded me of a project I had once started that I was calling my Experimental Novel and I decided that I wanted to attempt it again.

The Experiment, as I had come to think of it, is to write an entire novel in prose-poem form. Relying heavily on the thematic elements of the scene to convey the story. I decided that this would be like a silent animation or film, where the mood and 'music' of the scene tells the story. I was of course as many were, inspired by the beauty of the film Fantasia.

Will I complete it in the time frame of NaNoWriMo? Probably not. But it is going to be a fun little thing to play around with all the same.

Hoping you're able to keep safe and sane this holiday season.
